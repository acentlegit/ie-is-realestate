# US Real Estate - Regulatory Information

## State-Specific Real Estate Laws

### Common Regulations
- Disclosure requirements vary by state
- Property transfer taxes
- Recording fees
- Title insurance requirements
- Escrow procedures

### State-Level Variations
- California: Extensive disclosure requirements
- Texas: Different title procedures
- New York: Co-op/condo specific regulations
- Florida: Coastal property regulations

## Data Privacy & Compliance

### Data Usage Regulations
- Fair Housing Act compliance
- Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB) rules
- State privacy laws (CCPA, etc.)
- MLS data usage restrictions

### API Usage Compliance
- Census data: Public use, attribution required
- County data: Check individual portal terms
- Commercial APIs: Subscription terms apply
