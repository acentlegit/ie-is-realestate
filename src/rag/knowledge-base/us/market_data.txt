# US Real Estate - Market Data

## Market Data Sources

### Census Data
- Demographics by census tract
- Income levels (median household income)
- Housing characteristics
- Population density
- Education levels

### County Assessor Data
- Property assessments
- Tax records
- Sale history
- Property characteristics

### MLS Data
- Active listings
- Sold properties
- Days on market
- Price trends
- Inventory levels

### Market Indices
- Local market trends
- Regional price indices
- National housing indices
- Market condition indicators

## Data Schema

### Core Tables

**property**
- property_id, address, city, state, zip
- lat, lon, property_type
- year_built, sqft, beds, baths, lot_size

**listing**
- listing_id, property_id, source
- status, list_price, list_date
- days_on_market, description_text

**transaction**
- transaction_id, property_id
- sale_date, sale_price, deed_ref

**assessment_tax**
- property_id, tax_year
- assessed_value, property_tax

**permit**
- permit_id, property_id
- permit_date, permit_type, declared_value
